Our meeting was unlikely.

Having made our fair share of bad decisions (and some good), we would not have had a chance to meet; it was by luck, Jeremy’s birthday, and our friend Emily insisting on an introduction that it happened at all. We are, after all, not only her most attractive friends, but also her token liberal friends. She arranged our meeting and we clicked.

Even though neither of us wanted to leap into anything too quickly, we definitely found ourselves spending more time together than apart. A year later, with a “yes” to a certain question, we find ourselves here and couldn’t be happier.
